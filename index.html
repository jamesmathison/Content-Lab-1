Your Lab's Structure

Focused on KPIs

You can forget about dealing with scattered freelancers who only understand their craft.

Your Lab will be focused on the results you need. We will work with your to decide the most meaningful KPIs for your business, and revise them monthly.

Everything we publish for you will be tracked, and the metrics analysed and presented in easy to read reports. You’ll be able to see exactly what we’re doing for you, and what it’s doing for your business.

 

Immaculate Quality

The content analyst is not only analysing the effect of your content’s metrics. His creative side is also key. He’ll edit all content coming from the creators, so it only goes live when it’s elite.

More than just grammar and spelling, the content analyst will assess style, tone, narrative structure, logical clarity and much more.
